## The Framework

1. A product name that states the result and timeframe

## Key Principles

**Why the $27–47 price point holds:**  
Each product solves a specific, time-sensitive problem where the cost of *not* solving it dwarfs the price. The product removes friction *and* the need to figure out what to do with the information — that's the gap AI and Google don't fill.

**What makes them afternoon-buildable:**  
The deliverable is always a combination of a worksheet, a template, a script, or a checklist — not a course with 40 videos. You're packaging your decision-making process, not your knowledge.
