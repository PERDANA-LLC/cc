**💡 Tip for selling these:** The most successful transformation products always use the **"Result + Timeframe + Objection Handling"** formula. 
* *Result:* "Reduce your debt to zero"
* *Timeframe:* "in 90 days"
* *Objection/Pain point:* "without giving up your morning coffee or living on rice and beans." 

When you frame your product this way, you are selling the *destination*, not the airplane.
