## Phase 1 — Set up your Etsy seller account
*One-time setup — 20 min*

### Step 1 — Go to etsy.com and click "Sell on Etsy"
Create a free account if you don't have one. Use an email address you check regularly — Etsy sends order notifications here.

### Step 2 — Open your shop and choose your shop name
Go to: Account → Sell on Etsy → Open your Etsy shop. Pick a name that is clear and searchable.

Good name options for this product:
- BudgetWithClarity
- TheBudgetSystem
- MoneyFitsLife

> **Tip:** Your shop name cannot have spaces. Check availability at namecheckr.com first so you can match it across social media.

### Step 3 — Set your shop language, country, and currency
Choose English (US) even if you are outside the US — it gives you the largest buyer pool. Set your currency to USD since $27 is the listed price.

### Step 4 — Connect your payment method
Add a bank account or PayPal to receive payouts. On a $27 sale you keep approximately $24.55.

> **Tip:** Etsy Payments is the preferred method — it supports more buyer payment types and processes faster than PayPal-only shops.

## Phase 4 — Get your first sales
*Etsy organic is slow at first — these steps accelerate it*

### Step 16 — Turn on Etsy Ads with a $1/day budget
Go to: Shop Manager → Marketing → Etsy Ads

Set a **$1–3/day budget** and promote your listing. Etsy Ads give new shops immediate search visibility before organic ranking builds. Run for 2 weeks to get initial data on which keywords convert.

> **Tip:** You are spending $14–42 to get your first reviews. Reviews compound — a listing with 10 reviews converts at 3–5× the rate of one with zero.

### Step 17 — Share your listing link in 3 places today

1. **Reddit** — post in r/personalfinance or r/povertyfinance. Frame it as a resource, not an ad.
2. **Facebook groups** — find budgeting or money-saving groups.
3. **Instagram or Pinterest** — post your cover image with the listing link in bio.

Your first 5 sales will likely come from your own promotion, not Etsy search.

### Step 18 — Ask your first 3 buyers for a review

Etsy sends buyers an automatic review request. You can also send a manual follow-up message 3 days post-purchase:

> "Hi! Hope the workbook is helping. If you have 30 seconds, an honest review helps other people find it — and means the world to a small shop."

### Step 19 — Duplicate the listing to create 2–3 variations

Etsy ranks each listing independently. Duplicate your listing and change the title/tags to target different search terms:

- "Beginner Budget Workbook"
- "Monthly Budget Planner for Beginners"
- "Anti-Budget System PDF"

Same product, more search entry points.

> **Tip:** Each listing costs $0.20 to publish. Creating 3 listings = $0.60 investment for 3× the organic search surface area.

### Step 20 — Check your Etsy stats after 7 days and adjust

Go to: Shop Manager → Stats

| Metric | If low → fix this |
|--------|-------------------|
| Views | Tags and title (not being found) |
| Click-through rate | Cover image (not compelling) |
| Conversion rate | Description or price (not convincing) |
